Computer Accessories

Computer accessories are additional hardware components or peripheral devices that enhance the functionality, usability, or performance of a computer system. Whether you are a laptop user or a desktop user, computer accessories are important for enhancing user experience. Some common computer accessories include keyboards, mouse, mouse pads, webcams, speakers, headphones, pen drives, and power strips. These PC accessories allow you to customize your computer setup to better suit your specific needs. With more people relying on computers for remote work, online education, and digital entrepreneurship, having the right accessories can significantly impact efficiency and output quality. If you are looking to buy the best quality accessories for your desktop or laptop, Star Tech offers a diverse range of options from well-known brands at the best price in BD.

Computer Accessories Available in BD

Whether you're a casual user, a creative professional, or a hardcore gamer, there's a wide range of computer accessories available to suit your needs and preferences. Below are the various types of PC accessories available in Bangladesh:

Keyboard

When it comes to computer accessories, the keyboard is one of the most important peripherals that allows users to enter text, commands, and data into a computer. Popular brands like Logitech, A4Tech, Gamdias, Razer, Rapoo, Corsair, Redragon, and Keychron offer a wide range of keyboards in Bangladesh. You can choose from wired and wireless models and membrane or mechanical keyboards for gaming and the ultimate typing experience. Mechanical keyboards are available with different switch types, such as Cherry MX (Red, Blue, Brown), Gateron, and Kailh, offering varying levels of tactile feedback and actuation force. Gaming keyboards also offer customizable keys, RGB lighting effects, anti-ghosting, and dedicated macro buttons to cater to the needs of gamers.

Mouse

Mouse is another important computer accessory that enables users to navigate and control the on-screen cursor. Optical mouse and laser mouse are the most common types, offering smooth and responsive tracking on various surfaces. Wireless mouse provide freedom from cables, while gaming mouse comes with programmable buttons, adjustable DPI levels, and ergonomic shapes for enhanced gaming performance. Vertical mouse offer an enhanced ergonomic experience for comfortable hand positioning during extended use. Logitech, A4Tech, Razer, Gamdias, Gigabyte, Redragon, and other well-known accessories brands offer a diverse range of mouse to suit different needs and preferences in BD.

Headphones

Headphones are designed to provide an immersive and personal listening experience. Available in various form factors like over-ear, on-ear, and in-ear models, headphones can meet different preferences. In Bangladesh, users can choose from a wide range of headphone options from various accessories brands like JBL, Sony, Bose, Razer, Beats by Dr. Dre, Audio Technica, Beyerdynamic, and Anker. Noise-canceling headphones actively reduce ambient noise for a distraction-free audio experience, ideal for travel or noisy environments. Gaming headphones are equipped with surround sound capabilities, microphones for voice communication, and customizable EQ settings. Bluetooth Headphones provide freedom from tangled wires and offer portability.

Speakers and Home Theaters

Speakers are a must-have output device for a computer system whether you are watching movies, listening to music, or playing games. JBL, Sony, Samsung, Bose. Edifier, Microlab, F&D, and Beats by Dr, Dre offer a diverse range of speakers for various usage scenarios. Standalone speakers range from compact desktop units to powerful floor-standing models, offering rich and detailed sound reproduction. Home theater systems integrate a combination of multiple speakers, including a subwoofer for deep bass, and a receiver or amplifier to create a cinematic audio experience. On the other hand, Soundbars provide a convenient and space-saving alternative, delivering immersive surround sound without the need for multiple speakers. For those who are seeking a wireless tangle-free audio experience, Bluetooth Speakers are an optimal choice.

Webcam

Webcams are necessary for live streaming, video conferencing, and recording videos. These compact cameras can be integrated into laptops or desktops, or purchased separately for added flexibility. High-quality webcams offer features like wide-angle lenses, autofocus, low-light performance, and built-in microphones for seamless video and audio capture. Webcams have become very important accessories for remote work, online meetings, and content creation, providing a convenient way to connect and collaborate virtually. Brands like Logitech, A4Tech, Fantech, Havit, and Redragon offer a wide range of webcams for various needs and requirements.

Microphone

The microphone is one of the most essential accessories for podcasting, video production, music recording, and online meetings. Popular microphone brands in Bangladesh include Boya, Audio Technica, Rode, K&F Concept, Sennheiser, Neumann, and Fifine. These reputed brands offer various types of microphones for various audio requirements. Desktop microphones offer plug-and-play convenience and often feature built-in stands and adjustable pickup patterns. Condenser microphones are known for their superior sound quality and sensitivity, while dynamic microphones are suitable for live performances and loud environments. USB microphones provide a simple solution for direct computer connectivity, making them ideal for streaming, gaming, and online communication.

Cables and Converters

Cables and converters are essential accessories for connecting and transferring data, audio, and video signals between devices and peripherals. In Bangladesh, Star Tech offers a wide range of cable and converter options from popular brands to ensure seamless connectivity and compatibility. HDMI cables enable high-definition video and audio transmission, while USB cables and Type-C cables facilitate data transfer and power delivery. Ethernet cables provide wired internet connectivity, and audio cables transmit sound signals between components. Converters, such as DisplayPort to HDMI or USB-C to HDMI adapters, and card readers ensure compatibility between devices with different port types, enabling seamless connectivity and signal conversion.
