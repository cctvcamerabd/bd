Types of Gaming Headsets

There are variations among gaming headphones that can be found. A good quality gaming headphone can change your Gaming Desktop Setup aesthetics. The following types of gaming headsets are commonly used by consumers.

Wired & Wireless Gaming Headsets

Wired gaming headphones refer to headsets that can be connected to the Gaming PC through a wire. These headphones are comparatively less costly than wireless ones as they dont require batteries and Bluetooth. The connection is consistent. You can find a wide range of the best gaming headphones under 1000 BDT from Star Tech. However, wireless gaming headphones require Bluetooth to connect to a PC and batteries to charge with flexibility of movement.

Over-Ear Gaming Headphones

This type of headset is designed with high ear cups that cover the ears and provide a seamless immersive sound experience by creating barriers from exterior noise. This type of headset is more accepted among gamers as it adds depth to the Gaming PC setup. For comfort and the ability to provide strong audio, Over-Ear headsets are popular among players for long gaming sessions.

On-Ear Gaming Headphones

A gaming headset that is made with smaller ear cups that sit directly in the ears instead of fully enclosing them is called On-Ear headphones. On-ear gaming headphones offer portability, better audio quality, and comfort balance. It is suited to a wide range of environments to cater to diverse people.

In-Ear Gaming Earbuds

In-ear gaming earbuds are compact and lightweight headsets designed for gaming. They are suitable for use in the ear canal, which provides a portable and accessible solution to play games on the move or in situations where it may not be possible to use standard over-ear gaming headphones.

Noise-Cancelling Headphones

The Noise Canceling Gaming Headphones are manufactured to eliminate undesirable outside noises utilizing sophisticated technology. In particular, they are particularly effective at reducing the constant low-frequency sounds such as those of an airplane engine, fan noise, or a train's sound. Using microphones, these headsets can pick up external sounds and then create sound waves 180 degrees out of phase with the noise they've detected so that it can be effectively eliminated.
