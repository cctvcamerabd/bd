What is a Laptop Bag and is it necessary?

Laptop bags emerged from traditional office bags or briefcases and a laptop bag will always have a dedicated, padded compartment for protecting the laptop. Also, laptop bags and laptop backpacks will always be made of soft materials as opposed to some hard-shell briefcases. Laptop bags are also great for carrying other delicate electronics or simply traveling since they tend to be weather-sealed. Plus, because laptop bags are designed for the highest portability, organizing a laptop bag or laptop backpack is far more convenient.

Which type of Laptop Bag is Best?

The best type of laptop bag and backpack is the one that your device and health at the same time. In Bangladesh, there are three types of laptop bags available which are - Shoulder Laptop Bags, Laptop Backpack, and Laptop Cases or sleeves. Let's take a look at how each type differs from the other and what their unique benefits are.

Shoulder Laptop Bag

The shoulder laptop bag is the most popular type among laptop users, owing to its functional advantage over a laptop backpack and laptop sleeve. A shoulder laptop bag features a strap for hanging the bag from a shoulder, diagonally crossing the torso. Shoulder laptop bags are the most popular choice as laptop bags for men. For women, there are close versions of shoulder type, which are tote laptop bags. Theres also a close variant called messenger laptop bags, and these are equally desirable as a laptop bag for women.

Shoulder laptop bags are most sought after for greater maneuverability and accessibility. This type is more popular among young adults and professionals for its elegant looks that go with any environment. For quality shoulder laptop bags, the WiWu shop at Star Tech is a great place to start with. There are other brands like Tucano and MaxGreen which are also respectable for their price-to-performance ratio.

Laptop Sleeves & Cases

Sleeve laptop bags allow only tucking your laptop in the bag and typically nothing else. Laptop cases however are the ones that come with a little extra room for the absolutely necessary peripherals like the charger and maybe a pair of earphones or an SSD. Laptop sleeves and laptop cases come with no strap or handle whatsoever. Sleeve laptop bags may also come with a magnetic strip as a locking mechanism or zippers. These are best for short movement with your laptop or Tablet, just like how you do with an Apple folio cover. 

Laptop Backpacks

Laptop Backpacks are most famous for times when someone has to carry a laptop larger than 15 inches or Tab. Backpacks for laptops are also a popular choice for young and energetic minds and those who love to carry lots of gadgets. Laptop backpacks offer the most space to take your necessary equipment, clothes, and cosmetics. 

Features of a Good Laptop Bag or Backpack

If you are out for a good laptop bag or backpack, there are some crucial factors to look into before buying. The factors span from objective matters like material and quality to subjective things like color, comfort, and organizing the laptop bag or backpack. Lets go through them briefly below.

Quality and Build Material

The quality of a good laptop bag largely depends on the build material. Build material also plays a significant role in its durability, look, and feel. A Laptop bag and backpack can be made of nylon, leather, canvas, Tarpaulin, or TPE materials. Canvas and leather laptop bags, however, sit on premium shelves and give a classy look, and are comfortable to the touch. 

Waterproof Laptop Backpack

The most sought feature in a good laptop backpack is waterproofing. Waterproof laptop bags can be found in Bangladesh at Star Tech with enticing price tags. 13-inch to 15-inch Waterproof laptop bags & backpacks at our shop come with highly water-repellent technologies. You can find waterproof backpacks coated with DWR and Polyurethane or made of waxed canvas and roll-top zippers. Waterproof laptop bags with roll-top zippers are ideal for any formal or casual environment.

False Bottom

One of the features of any laptop bag or backpack you want to make sure of is that it has a false bottom. The false bottoms in a laptop bag or laptop backpack are an added cushion at the bottom of it. This protects your expensive device from accidental shock by absorbing impact. Brands like Targus, Tucano, and Cougar manufacture good quality 15-inch laptop bags and large laptop backpacks of above 10 liters which are available at our online shop.

Organizing The Laptop Bag

Finally, make sure that you are able to easily organize your laptop bag or laptop backpack and its best to do that at the shop while checking out the bag. You can organize your laptop bag and backpack by using additional pouches, modular organizer bags, velcro straps, and many others. 

Screen Safe Mesh & Straps

While purchasing organizers for laptop bags, ensure that they are safe for the screen and wont scratch it. Extended use can cause fogging up the laptop screen with tiny scratches from the bags inside the wall, mesh, straps, etc. Ensuring screen-safe mesh and straps for organizing the laptop bag will significantly reduce wear and tear issues of the device.
