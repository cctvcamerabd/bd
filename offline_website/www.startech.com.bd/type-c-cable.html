Benefits of Using Type-C Cables

Type-C cables offer significant advantages in the world of connectivity with fast charging and fast data transfer speed compared to other data cables. Here are the benefits that you can get by using USB Type-C cables:

Fast Charging

Type-C cables support faster charging speeds compared to older USB types. USB Type-C Cables are designed to support higher power delivery standards, such as USB Power Delivery (PD), and are capable of delivering up to 240 watts of power. Type-C allows you to quickly charge your phone, tablets, and other gadgets.

Faster Data Transfer

Type-C cables also support faster data transfer speeds than older USB cables. With support for USB 3.1 Gen 1, Type-C cables can offer data transfer speeds of up to 5 Gbps, while USB 3.1 Gen 2 can go up to 10 Gbps. USB 3.2 Gen 2x2 can deliver data transfer speeds of up to 20 Gbps, and the newer USB4 standard can reach up to 40 Gbps.

Reversible Connector

USB Type-C cables come with an oval-shaped connector design and can be plugged in either way. The symmetrical connector of USB-C Cables is a major advantage over older USB connectors.

Universal Compatibility

Type-C has become the new standard and is being used in various devices, including smartphones, tablets, laptops, desktop computers, monitors, external hard drives, peripherals like keyboards and mice, and even some cameras. This means that you can use one cable to charge and sync all of your devices.

Things to Consider When Buying a Type-C Cable

There are several important things to consider when buying a Type-C cable. Choose the best-suited option based on your specific needs and requirements:

Cable Length

When buying a Type-C cable, you may want to determine the length of the cable based on your specific requirements. USB-C cables are offered in 0.5m, 1m, 1.2m, 1.5m, 1.8m, 2m, and 3-meter lengths in Bangladesh. Choose a Type-C cable that is long enough for your needs.

Material

Look for high-quality Type-C cables as they are made from durable materials to ensure longevity and reliability. There are many options available in the market like PVC, TPE, Nylon Braided, and even Metal Braided Type-C cables. Also, the connectors and housing may be made with aluminum alloy to provide extra durability.

Thunderbolt vs. Type-C Cable

Type-C and Thunderbolt often get mixed up with each other due to their identical physical connectors, but they have significant differences. Type-C is primarily a connector type, while Thunderbolt is a combination of connector and protocol, offers higher performance. Thunderbolt cables offer faster data transfer speeds up to 40 Gbps compared to Type-C cable's maximum of 20 Gbps. Type-C cables are Ideal for everyday tasks like charging, data transfer, and connecting monitors. Thunderbolt cables are best suited for professionals needing top-tier performance and multiple monitor setups. Those who own an Apple MacBook, Dell XPS, Razer Blade, Microsoft Surface Laptop Studio, or Apple Mac mini require Thunderbolt cables for faster connectivity.
